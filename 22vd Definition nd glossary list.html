Today's topic is- Definition list & Glossary list

To describe or to define a term in a webpage, this type of tagging is used for. Here we have 2 terms to define. At first, simple definition and then we will use definition listing.
1. Cryptocurrency
A digital currency in which transactions are verified and records maintained by a decentralized system using cryptography, rather than by a centralized authority.
2. Cryptography
The art of writing or solving codes.

Now these two above in the form of definition list-

Cryptocurrency
A digital currency in which transactions are verified and records maintained by a decentralized system using cryptography, rather than by a centralized authority.
Cryptography
The art of writing or solving codes.

Remeber that
  • dt (define-TERM):tag is use to define a term in the list
  • dd (define-DESCRIPTION):tag is use to describe a term in the list
format:
       dl-
             dt- (your term/ product name to be defined) - /dt
             dd - definition and description of the term/ product -/dd-
      /dl
-Write dt & dd tag in the same line.
-Repeat this dt & dd for all your products.
-Furthermore, to enlarge your terms you can use heading tag list if you want to.
