Todays topic is- Paragraph

1. Use of p | It is used to-
It is recommended that we will always use any of the tag in the page.
Paragraph tag has not any automated br tag. It need no line breaking tag!
Paragraph tag does not take automated line breake with "Enter"

2. Use of & n b s p | It is used to-
In general, browser takes only one space for once, even if, you use 20spaces!
It is used to show forced space in between letters.
& n b s p has no ending or starting tag
It is just it is!! See the examples on top of this page,
Use it to leave some blank spaces! Good luck.

3. Use of adress | It is used to-
It is used to give a Local Address on the page.
Remember: This tag has no other commands. It just shows an adress to the reader
You will have to determine line breaking
Exp:

MD Alal 283-6, example lane, white house Shiddhirganj, Narayanganj. Banglades
