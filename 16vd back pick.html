Today's topic is- Creating a background picture for a webpage

Format background = "photoName.format"
                    - use this format inside the start of the body tag

Before you start-
Webpages support 3types of format. Formats are-
                    - .jpg format.
                    - .gif format.
                    - .png format.


Before you create-
                    - It's better you have the photo illustration knowledge.
                    - Check out the photo detail format.
                    - Keep your photos, HTML files, other related staffs in the same folder in your drive.
